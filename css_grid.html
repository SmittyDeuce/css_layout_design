Dragon Ball Z

Saiyan Race

The Saiyan race is the race of the main protagonists in the show, including Goku/Kakarot, Vegeta, and Broly, who are the only full-blooded Saiyans left alive. Gohan, Goten (sons of Goku), and Trunks (son of Vegeta) are half-human, while Pan (granddaughter of Goku) is 1/4 Saiyan. The Saiyans were originally a warrior race known for their incredible fighting abilities and battle-hardened nature. They were almost entirely wiped out by Frieza, a powerful tyrant, who feared the potential threat they posed. Bred for combat, Saiyans were known for being cold-hearted warriors who thrived on the thrill of battle and sought to increase their strength. They often conquered planets, selling them to the highest bidder, and those with higher battle power held higher social status within their society. Goku, raised on Earth, became an exception, developing a kind and compassionate nature that set him apart from the typical Saiyan demeanor all thanks to falling on his head as an infant.

Saiyan Transformations

Saiyans possess the unique ability to transform, drastically increasing their power levels. The most well-known transformation is the Super Saiyan form, characterized by golden hair and a significant power boost. Goku was the first Saiyan to achieve this form in centuries, during his battle with Frieza.

Other transformations include Super Saiyan 2, where the user's power and speed are further amplified, and Super Saiyan 3, which features even more pronounced physical changes and a massive increase in power. In the Dragon Ball Super series, new forms like Super Saiyan God and Super Saiyan Blue have been introduced, pushing the limits of Saiyan power even further.

Each transformation is typically triggered by intense emotion or a need to surpass one's limits, highlighting the Saiyan's warrior nature and their potential for growth.

Character Profiles

Goku

Summary: Goku, also known as Kakarot, is the main protagonist of Dragon Ball Z. He is a Saiyan who was sent to Earth as a baby and raised as a human. Goku is known for his cheerful personality, insatiable appetite, and incredible fighting abilities. He achieves numerous transformations and power-ups throughout the series, including Super Saiyan forms.

Last Listed Power Level: 90,000 (scouter reading during the Saiyan Saga)

Vegeta

Summary: Vegeta, the Prince of all Saiyans, initially appears as a ruthless antagonist but gradually becomes one of Goku's closest allies. Known for his pride and fierce rivalry with Goku, Vegeta is a formidable fighter who also achieves various transformations and power-ups. He is one of the most determined and powerful Saiyans.

Last Listed Power Level: 18,000 (scouter reading during the Saiyan Saga)

Gohan

Summary: Gohan is Goku’s eldest son and a key character in Dragon Ball Z. Initially introduced as a young and timid child, Gohan grows into a powerful fighter, especially during the Cell Saga. He is known for his latent power and eventual role as the savior of Earth during the Cell Games.

Last Listed Power Level: 1,300 (scouter reading during the Saiyan Saga)

Trunks

Summary: Trunks is the son of Vegeta and Bulma, and he plays a significant role in the Android and Cell Sagas. Trunks is known for traveling back in time to warn the Z Fighters about future threats. He is a skilled fighter and achieves notable power-ups, including Super Saiyan transformations.

Last Listed Power Level: 1,000 (scouter reading during the Android Saga)

Pan

Summary: Pan is the granddaughter of Goku and the daughter of Gohan. She is a young but incredibly talented fighter with a strong sense of justice. Despite her young age, Pan shows great promise and potential in combat.

Last Listed Power Level: N/A (Power level not measured with scouters)

Goten

Summary: Goten is the younger son of Goku and the younger brother of Gohan. Like his brother, Goten is a skilled fighter and quickly achieves the Super Saiyan transformation at a very young age. He is known for his cheerful personality and strong bond with Trunks.

Last Listed Power Level: 8,000 (scouter reading during the Buu Saga)

Broly

Summary: Broly is a Saiyan who is known for his immense power and uncontrollable rage. Introduced in the Dragon Ball Z movies, he is considered one of the most powerful Saiyans, with his legendary Super Saiyan form giving him extraordinary strength. Broly’s power level is significantly higher than most other Saiyans.

Last Listed Power Level: N/A (Power level not measured with scouters)

Bardock

Summary: Bardock is the father of Goku and a Saiyan warrior who fought bravely against Frieza’s forces. He is known for his strong sense of justice and his battle against Frieza’s forces just before the destruction of Planet Vegeta. Bardock's power level is high compared to other Saiyan warriors of his time.

Last Listed Power Level: 10,000 (scouter reading during the Frieza Saga)

Raditz

Summary: Raditz is Goku’s older brother and a Saiyan warrior who initially arrives on Earth as an antagonist. He is known for his ruthless nature and high power level. Raditz's arrival marks the beginning of significant events in Dragon Ball Z.

Last Listed Power Level: 1,500 (scouter reading during the Saiyan Saga)

Nappa

Summary: Nappa is a Saiyan warrior and a former ally of Vegeta. He is known for his brutish strength and is one of the primary antagonists during the Saiyan Saga. Nappa's combat abilities make him a formidable opponent.

Last Listed Power Level: 4,000 (scouter reading during the Saiyan Saga)

King Vegeta

Summary: King Vegeta is the former King of the Saiyan race and Vegeta's father. He ruled Planet Vegeta before its destruction by Frieza. King Vegeta was a powerful Saiyan, known for his strength and leadership.

Last Listed Power Level: 20,000 (scouter reading before Planet Vegeta's destruction)
